Grilled Cheese

Description

What's there to say about grilled cheese? Gooey, melty cheese sandwiched between two crisp slices of toast, what more could you ask for? Dive in to this simple yet delicious, timeless recipe that's sure to leave a smile on your face, and an empty plate in front of you.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Mayo
  • American cheese
  • Shredded cheddar
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your pan over medium heat
  2. Slather a thin layer of mayo over one side of a slice of bread
  3. Lay it mayo-side down in the pan, then add a slice of American cheese
  4. Add a sprinkle of cheddar cheese to the top
  5. Sprinkle italian seasoning on top
  6. Wait for the cheese to melt, and the bread to toast
  7. Once toasted, add another slice of mayo-slathered bread on top, mayo side up this time
  8. Using a spatula, flip the sandwich over
  9. Allow the second slice of bread to brown
  10. Once browned, remove from pan
  11. Enjoy!
