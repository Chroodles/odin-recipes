Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo, a timeless Italian classic that gained even more popularity in the United States. Butter, garlic, cream, pasta and chicken...what's not to love? In this recipe, we'll be crafting the perfect comfort meal, in just a few quick steps!

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccini
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, paprika)

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. Season your chicken with salt, pepper and paprika
  3. Melt the butter over medium heat, then add garlic to the pan, cooking until fragrant (about 1 minute)
  4. Add heavy cream, stirring frequently. Bring it to a simmer then reduce heat
  5. Add fresh grated parmesan, stirring frequently
  6. While sauce cooks, bring a pot of water to a boil, cooking the fettuccini until al-dente
  7. While pasta and sauce cook, heat olive oil in a pan, then sear seasoned chicken in a pan over medium heat
  8. Once everything finishes, pour sauce over pasta
  9. Slice chicken crosswise, laying it atop the pasta
  10. Enjoy!
