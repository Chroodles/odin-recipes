Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Description

Imagine this: A cold winter day, snuggled up in some blankets, a steaming bowl of creamy, decadent broccoli cheddar soup in your lap. Well, now you don't have to imagine! (Well...unless it's not winter I guess) Follow this recipe and you'll soon be in a world of cheesy, broccoli goodness!

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Matchstick carrots
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, garlic powder)
  • Chicken broth
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened. Stir in broccoli and matchstick carrots. Add broth and simmer until broccoli is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Reduce heat; add shredded cheddar and stir until melted. Stir in milk and seasonings.
  3. Stir frequently, until thick.
